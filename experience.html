Hey. Still Kyle.

A brief rundown of my experience.

Teacher

For a little over 20 years, I taught various social studies class at both the middle and high school levels.

I taught a variety of subjects from Geography and Ethics to History and Economics.

Although I've been out of the classroom for a decade or so, I still have an active YouTube channel devoted to demonstrations in Economics.

Administrator

I took my first administrative job in 2004, working as an assistant principal.

Since 2004, I've been a principal at both the middle school and high school levels.

Technology Integration

I moved into tech three years ago, working as something called a "technology integration specialist," a fancy term for someone in a school who helps students, teachers, administrators, and parents to make better use technology.

Last year I worked with over 150 students and teachers, and I hope to work with even more this year.
